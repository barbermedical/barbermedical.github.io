Downloads

My notes are free to download and use. It helps a lot if you download direct from the site and ask your colleagues to do the same! Please don’t resell them or claim them as your own.


The structure of these notes was heavily inspired by Scott's Notes. The content was put together by me between 2020 and 2022 using updated resources, guidelines, and literature. They are aimed primarily for clinical year medical students.


There are no notes for surgery as I had excellent other resources to use and time is finite.

How to Use These Notes

These notes are meant to support your learning, not replace your textbooks or lectures. Medicine is always changing, these may not be fully up to date.
