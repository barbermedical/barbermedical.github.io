Medical Student Notes

My notes are free to download and use. It helps a lot if you download direct from the site and ask your colleagues to do the same! Please don’t resell them or claim them as your own.


The structure of these notes was heavily inspired by Scott's Notes. The content was put together by me between 2020 and 2022 using updated resources, guidelines, and literature. These notes are intended for medical students in the penultimate and final years of Irish/UK medical degrees, but they should also be appropriate for students at the equivalent stage in other medical programmes worldwide.


There are no notes for surgery as I had excellent other resources to use and time is finite.
