Welcome

My name is Seán Barber. I enjoy learning and sharing what I learn. On this site you’ll find a bit about me, what I’m working on, and a collection of free notes you can download.

About Me

I'm a junior doctor from Cork, Ireland. I graduated from University College Cork School of Medicine in 2022. I currently work in anaesthetics & intensive care in Melbourne, Australia. I wrote extensive notes for my own final medical exams, and these are freely available for download.


I aim to train in anaesthesia and expand my involvement in medical education.

Highlights & Interests

Anaesthesia & Critical Care

My professional interest lies in anaesthesia and intensive care. I am currently a senior resident in anaesthesia in Northern Health, Victoria, gaining valuable experience.

Education

I have been involved in education since starting my medical degree with tutoring, class delivery, and material and assessment design. I have completed a master's degree in medical education based on research in medical student admissions. My medical student notes have been freely available for use since my own graduation.
